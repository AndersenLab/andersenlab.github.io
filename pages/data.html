QX1430 x CB4856 RIAILs

Our new collection of advanced intercross recombinant inbred lines (RIAILs) is described in Andersen et al. QX1430 contains the natural allele of npr-1 and a transposon insertion into peel-1. This strain is more compatible with strains containing N2-like zeel-1 peel-1 and eliminates the large physiological effects caused by npr-1. The data below include:

  1. Genotypes for these RIAILs (QX240-QX598) - FileS1
  2. Phenotypes from our high-throughput fitness assays - FileS2
  3. All QTL from Andersen et al. G3 2015 - FileS3
  4. Plots of all QTL from Andersen et al. G3 2015 - FileS4

RIAILs

The following binary R data file can be used in R/qtl to map traits of interest using the Rockman et al. RIAILs. It contains: a N2xCB4856 (QX1-QX239) dummy cross object, a N2xCB4856 map object, and a phenotype merging function.

  • N2xCB4856_RIAILs_Rqtlfiles.RData
