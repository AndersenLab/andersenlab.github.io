N2xCB4856 RIAILs (QX1-QX239)

We can distribute RIAILs from Rockman et al. Please contact Erik with a short description of your assay and needs. In most cases, we send 20 RIAILs, our N2 and CB4856 parents, and npr-1 near isogenic lines to (1) determine heritability and (2) rule out npr-1 as a likely causative locus. Please supply shipping information.

QX1430xCB4856 RIAILs (QX240-QX598)

We can distribute RIAILs from Andersen et al. Please contact Erik with a short description of your assay and needs. In most cases, we send 20 RIAILs along with QX1430 and CB4856 parents to determine heritability. Please supply shipping information.

CaeNDR

CaeNDR provides these three services:

  1. Collection, maintenance, and distribution of wild Caenorhabditis selfing species
  2. Organization and dissemination of whole-genome sequences for wild C. briggsae, C. elegans, and C. tropicalis strains
  3. Facilitation of genetic mappings to empower individual researchers in gene discovery
