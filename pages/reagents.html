QX1430xCB4856 RIAILs (QX240-QX598)

We can distribute RIAILs from Andersen et al. Please contact Erik with a short description of your assay and needs. In most cases, we send 20 RIAILs along with QX1430 and CB4856 parents to determine heritability. Please supply shipping information.

N2xCB4856 RIAILs (QX1-QX239)

We can distribute RIAILs from Rockman et al. Please contact Erik with a short description of your assay and needs. In most cases, we send 20 RIAILs, our N2 and CB4856 parents, and npr-1 nearly isogenic lines to (1) determine heritability and (2) rule out npr-1 as a likely causative locus. Please supply shipping information.

97 C. elegans isotypes from throughout the world

We can distribute the 97 isotypes from Andersen, Gerke, Shapiro et al. As with the RIAILs, please contact Erik with a short description of your assay, needs and your shipping information. Alternatively, most strains can be found at the CGC
